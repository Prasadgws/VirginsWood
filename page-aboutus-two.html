Who we are ?

We are a professional design firm started by young entrepreneurs building on design obsession and a vision that every home deserves to be a designer home and every person deserves designer living. A design talent powerhouse with high calibre design experts from across Indian and International design schools. A team of 200+ passionate individuals, we have a cumulative experience of 400 man-years of design & delivery. 1500 homes delivered in last 7 years across the city of Bengaluru while garnering praises, awards and experience across various typologies, sizes and themes. Full service offering from conceptualization to design, planning, visualization and project management with utmost care of personalization and quality delivery.

Methodology in our Madness

How do we works ?

As the vision is strong and the aim is high we are on a hunt for the creators, creators of design in interiors who are in search of a platform to express themselves.

Welcome To Your

You are welcomed into the Bonito family and are on the way to realizing your dream home. You can start visualizing with your designer after an initial payment fee of Rs. 0/-

Getting to Know You

Our designers get to know you and transform your ideas into a tangible reality. Complete project schematics with the timeline is shared

Design Phase

Our dedicated team of designers and mentors work to get all the details for your home right. The final 3D design cut is shown 10 days after the initiation of design.

Production Phase

YOur experts start procuring all materials for the production work to begin at the factory. Our artisans complete working and get ready for installation after 10 days

Installation Phase

All the products are dispatched from the factory and vendors which reach your home. The installation process begins and is completed with a Quality Assurance check.

Handover

Once all the products are received and installed at the site, we conduct a site inspection just to ensure everything is up to the mark. After that, we sign a formal handover document and your fully furnished home is handed over along with warranty and service letter.

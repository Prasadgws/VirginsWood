About us

We are the leader
in the Interior Services

Morden space and Virgins wood (MSVW) Interior Services is a venture, that will offer interior design services for homes and offices in the Bangalore area.

MSVW Interior Services also will provide end to end design and development solution including furniture, both new and antique, decorator fabric for home and office. Apart from that organization also support their clients for plumbing, false ceiling, lighting and all other interiors related needs. This venture offers the most personalized services. The company was formed in Aug 2009 in Bangalore. In the last 11 years completed more than 650 apartment interiors in Bangalore and around 80 other part of India. company has also completed 7 projects in Paris, France.
